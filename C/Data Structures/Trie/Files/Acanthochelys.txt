
acanthochelys is a genus of turtles the spiny swamp turtles in the family chelidae subfamily chelinae found in south 
america until recently the species of this genus were considered to be members of the genus platemys but were moved to 
the resurrected genus originally described by gray based on the type species by monotypy acanthochelys spixii taxonomy
